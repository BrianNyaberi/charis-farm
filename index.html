About Charis
Farm and company

Charis farm is located in Biashara Ward (behind Nyondia shopping) Centre, Naivasha Sub-County in Nakuru County, approximately 3 km from Nairobi- Nakuru highway. It was established in the year 2020 with few ornamental birds which has passionately grown into a center of interest for education and tourism not only in the region, but also countrywide. The farm being in pre-urban area, sits on a Â¼ acre of land which has been well utilized in rearing of:

  • Ornamental Birds.
  • Dairy goat farming.
  • Rabbit farming.
  • Aqua culture farming.
  • Organic urban farming.
  • Agri- business trainings.

This has seen the farm gain Agricultural Show of Kenya (ASK) Trophy Award of July 2022, for Best Brahma (Ornamental chicken) and territorial birds.
